Research basis

Shannon's channel-capacity result connects usable data rate to bandwidth and signal-to-noise ratio. The FCC opened 1,200 MHz from 5.925 to 7.125 GHz for unlicensed use in the United States, creating room for more wide channels. IEEE documents the progression from 160 MHz channels in Wi-Fi 6 to 320 MHz channels in Wi-Fi 7.
